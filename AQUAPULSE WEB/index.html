Water Saving Tips

Fix Leaks Promptly

A small drip can waste 20 gallons of water per day. Use AquaPulse to report leaks quickly.

Use Water-Saving Fixtures

Install low-flow showerheads and faucets to reduce water usage by up to 50%.

Collect Rainwater

Use barrels to collect rainwater for gardening and cleaning purposes.

Water Plants Wisely

Water plants early in the morning or late evening to minimize evaporation.
